About Distant Views B&B

Distant Views Bed & Breakfast is a stunning retreat located in the heart of Fredericksburg, Texas. The property boasts breathtaking hilltop mountain views that can be enjoyed for miles, making it the perfect destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Guests can take in the serene surroundings from the comfort of their private balcony or patio, or explore the property's many outdoor spaces, including a zip-line deer feeder.

In addition to its natural beauty, Distant Views Bed & Breakfast is also conveniently located near downtown Fredericksburg, where visitors can find an array of shops and restaurants. Whether you're in the mood for a casual bite or a fine dining experience, there's something for everyone in this charming Texas town. Plus, the B&B is close proximity to wineries and tasting rooms.
